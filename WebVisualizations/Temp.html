Max Temperature

The plot shows an usual behavior, following the season of the year on each hemisphere. It can be observed on the plot that the southest the city, higher the Max Temperature will be. In contrast, the northest the city is, lower Max Temperature will be. Also, its important to highlight the equator zone, which mantains a quite constant trend of Max Temperature, from the -20 to the 20 latitude. Also, there are some outliers through the trend, which might be related to some meteorological phenomenons not related to the season of the year.
