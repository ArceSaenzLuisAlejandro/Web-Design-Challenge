Humidity

The plot shows almost no correlation between the humidity and the latitude of the city. In the south hemisphere, as expected, there are some cities with high humidity percentage; but this trend is not constant through the rest of the cities. Meanwhile, in the north hemisphere there's no correlation at all and also there's a group of outliers in the 70 latitude; those outliers might be there because of an atypical situation on the area.
